Step 1: Select Your Program Category

Start by selecting the program category that matches your educational goals:

Degree Programs

Bachelor's degrees from all Kenyan universities

Diploma Programs

Professional and technical diplomas

Certificate Programs

Short-term skill-based certifications

KMTC Programs

Medical and health sciences courses

Note: Each category has different subject requirements. Make sure you enter all your KCSE subjects for accurate matching.

Step 2: Enter Your KCSE Grades

After selecting your category, you'll need to enter your KCSE subject grades:

  • Mathematics, English, Kiswahili - Core compulsory subjects
  • Sciences - Chemistry, Biology, Physics (at least two sciences)
  • Humanities - History, Geography, CRE (at least one)
  • Technical Subjects - Optional, but can improve your chances
Important: Double-check your grades before submitting. Incorrect entries will lead to inaccurate results.
